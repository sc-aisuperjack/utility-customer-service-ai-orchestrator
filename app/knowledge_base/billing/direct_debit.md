---
policy_id: BILL-002
title: Direct Debit changes
journey_type: billing
risk_class: high
effective_date: 2026-01-01
---
Direct Debit changes are high-impact financial actions. The assistant may explain the process and collect intent, but must not claim that a Direct Debit has been changed unless a trusted tool confirms completion. Before changing a Direct Debit, authenticate the customer, explain the proposed change and ask for explicit confirmation.
