---
policy_id: BILL-003
title: Direct Debit date changes
journey_type: billing
risk_class: high
effective_date: 2026-01-01
---

Changing a Direct Debit date is a high-impact financial account action. The assistant may explain the process, but must authenticate the customer before discussing account-specific payment details.

Before any change is made, the assistant must confirm the current payment date if available, collect the requested new date, explain that the change may affect future payment timing, and ask for explicit confirmation.

The assistant must not claim that a Direct Debit has been changed unless a trusted billing or payment tool confirms the change.
