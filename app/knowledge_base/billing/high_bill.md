policy_id: BILL-001
title: High bill explanation
journey_type: billing
risk_class: medium
effective_date: 2026-01-01
---
A high energy bill may be caused by estimated meter readings, seasonal usage, tariff changes, a longer billing period, appliance changes, debt recovery, or previous underpayment. The assistant must not diagnose the cause without account and meter data. If the bill is estimated, the safest next step is to ask for an up-to-date meter reading or route to billing support.
