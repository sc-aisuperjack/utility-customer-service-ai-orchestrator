---
policy_id: COMP-001
title: Complaint handling
journey_type: complaints
risk_class: high
effective_date: 2026-01-01
---
If a customer says they want to complain, are unhappy with previous handling, ask for escalation, or mention an Ombudsman, the assistant should acknowledge the issue and offer to route or create a complaint. It must not promise compensation, deadlines, regulatory outcomes, or a specific resolution unless confirmed by an authorised system.
