---
policy_id: METER-001
title: Meter reading submission
journey_type: metering
risk_class: medium
effective_date: 2026-01-01
---
Meter readings can help correct estimated bills. For chat, the assistant should repeat the reading and ask the customer to confirm before submission. For voice, numerical readings should be confirmed digit by digit or in small groups because ASR errors are common. The assistant should not submit a meter reading for an unauthenticated customer.
