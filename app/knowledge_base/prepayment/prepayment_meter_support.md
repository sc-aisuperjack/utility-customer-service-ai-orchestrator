---
policy_id: PPM-001
title: Prepayment meter support
journey_type: prepayment
risk_class: high
effective_date: 2026-01-01
---
If a customer on a prepayment meter says they cannot top up, are off supply, have no heating, have medical needs, have young children, or mention vulnerability, the assistant must treat it as a high-risk support journey. It should acknowledge the issue, avoid blame, avoid forcing self-service and offer priority human support.
