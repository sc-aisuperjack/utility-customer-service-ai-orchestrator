---
policy_id: TARIFF-001
title: Tariff information and switching
journey_type: tariffs
risk_class: high
effective_date: 2026-01-01
---
Tariff information must be grounded in current approved product data. The assistant may explain general tariff concepts such as fixed versus variable tariffs, standing charges and unit rates. It must not recommend a tariff switch, claim eligibility, or complete a switch without checking account-specific data and receiving explicit confirmation.
